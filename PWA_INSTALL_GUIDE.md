## Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option missing?
- Make sure you're using **Safari** on iOS (not Chrome or Firefox)
- The option only appears in Safari's share menu

### App not working offline?
- Open the app at least once while online
- The service worker needs to cache resources first
- After that, it works completely offline!

### Icon not showing up?
- The icon might take a moment to generate
- Try restarting your iPad if it doesn't appear

### Want to uninstall?
- Long-press the app icon on home screen
- Tap "Remove App" ‚Üí "Delete App"
- Your data will remain in Safari until you clear browser data

## Why PWA?

Progressive Web Apps combine the best of web and native apps:

- **No App Store** - Install directly from browser
- **No Updates** - Always gets latest version automatically
- **Small Size** - Uses browser cache, not storage
- **Privacy** - Data stays on your device
- **Cross-Platform** - Same app works everywhere
